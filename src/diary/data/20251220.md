text: |
    I bought Naoki Saito’s art book, "Nariwai"!
    I decided to get it because I’d run out of ideas for backgrounds and compositions, and I couldn't come up with anything on my own.
    So, I thought I’d take a deep dive into the work of the illustrator we all know from YouTube (his tutorial videos are so helpful, aren't they?). 
    His art has such crisp colors and a lively atmosphere that just looking at it makes me feel cheerful. 
    In terms of Senran Kagura, I think his style would really suit characters like Hibari and Minori.
    I hope I can incorporate the essence of his style into my own work in a way that blends well with Yaegashi Nan’s art style.
